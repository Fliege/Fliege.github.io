White Oak Barrels

White Oak Barrels formed in late 2011 as an acoustic duo with folk roots and musings towards writing recording and playing. Through various change-overs the band gained and lost members in a state of constant flux. After several name-changes and the loss of Carlos, the drummer, the band recruited Lead guitarist Max Fliegner . We play folk inspired rock with funky rhythms, and we do it damn well.

Timmy O'Toole

Timmy is the founder of the band and kind of bandleader, even he doesn't want to be. He love it to play rhythm on his beauty black guitar and funky riffs. In addition to his qualities as guitarist he is also amazing singer and "dancer".

Brendan Cuffe

Brendan is the freaky jumping bass-guitarist of the WOB. Sometimes he plays more notes than the guitarist and has a lot more movement on stage as the rest of the band together. His funky influence is one of the reasons for the catchy rhythm styl. When he is singing the second vocal line he never needs a microphone.

Klaus Miller

Klaus, the spanish drummer with the german name living in Dublin, is the man with sticks who is responsible for the beat. The little latino has the rhythm in his blood and stay in time like a metronom. In addition he is the designer of the band and create all the art works.

Max Fliegner

Max is the german guitarist of the band. He is not the man with the amazing rhythm but he is the one with the genius sound and feeling for melodies. Of course Max has his german attitude on stage, smiling and jumping is not everyones styl.
